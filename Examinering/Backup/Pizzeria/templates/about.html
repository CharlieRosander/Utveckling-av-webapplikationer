About The Perfect Slice

The Perfect Slice is a pizza restaurant that has been in business for 20 years. We have a wide variety of pizzas, including vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options. We also have a variety of drinks, including soda, beer, and wine. We are located in Kista, Sweden, and we are open from 9am-5pm, Monday-Friday. We are closed on weekends and holidays.

Our pizzas are made with the freshest ingredients, and we use only the best quality cheese and sauce. We also use only the best quality meats and vegetables. We have a wide variety of toppings, including pepperoni, sausage, mushrooms, onions, peppers, and more. We also have a variety of specialty pizzas, including Hawaiian, BBQ chicken, and more. We also have a variety of desserts, including brownies, cookies, and more.
